# MediCore General Hospital — Neurology Department

## Overview
The Neurology Department specialises in the diagnosis and treatment of
disorders of the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles.  Our
neurologists work closely with neurosurgeons, neuroradiologists, and
rehabilitation specialists to provide integrated patient care.

## Conditions We Treat
- Epilepsy and seizure disorders
- Stroke and transient ischaemic attack (TIA)
- Multiple sclerosis (MS) and other demyelinating diseases
- Parkinson's disease and other movement disorders
- Headache and migraine syndromes
- Peripheral neuropathy
- Alzheimer's disease and other dementias
- Neuromuscular diseases (ALS, myasthenia gravis)
- Sleep disorders (narcolepsy, restless leg syndrome)

## Diagnostic Services
- Electroencephalography (EEG) and video-EEG monitoring
- Electromyography (EMG) and nerve conduction studies (NCS)
- Lumbar puncture and CSF analysis
- Neuroimaging review in partnership with Radiology (MRI, CT, PET)
- Neuropsychological testing
