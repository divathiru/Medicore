MediCore General Hospital — Patient Billing & Insurance Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025
Version: 2.1

1. CONSULTATION FEES

   Standard outpatient consultation (GP):           $80
   Specialist outpatient consultation:              $150
   Emergency department consultation (non-trauma):  $200
   Emergency department consultation (trauma):      $350
   Inpatient daily room rate (general ward):        $400/day
   Inpatient daily room rate (private room):        $750/day
   Intensive Care Unit (ICU) daily rate:           $1,800/day

2. ACCEPTED INSURANCE PANELS

   MediCore is a panel provider for the following insurers:
   - HealthFirst Premier
   - BlueCross National
   - MediShield Plus
   - CareAssure Group
   - NationalHealth Cooperative

   Patients covered by a panel insurer must present their insurance card
   at registration.  Co-payments as defined in each policy apply.

3. SELF-PAY PATIENTS

   Self-pay patients are required to pay a deposit equal to the estimated
   cost of treatment at the time of admission.  A 10% prompt-payment
   discount is available for full settlement within 7 days of discharge.

4. CASHIER HOURS

   Main cashier counter: Monday – Saturday, 7:30 AM – 8:00 PM
   Emergency cashier (24h): adjacent to ED triage, open round the clock.

5. BILLING DISPUTES

   All billing disputes must be submitted in writing to the Billing
   Department within 30 days of invoice date.  Disputes can be sent to:
   billing@medicore.dev

6. QUEUE & PAYMENT PROCESSING

   Appointments are assigned a queue number by the Cashier upon payment
   confirmation.  Patients should retain their queue ticket and proceed
   to the relevant department waiting area.  SMS notification is sent
   when queue position reaches number 3.

7. OUTSTANDING BALANCES

   Unpaid balances older than 60 days may be referred to MediCore's
   collections partner.  Payment plans can be arranged — contact the
   cashier counter or email billing@medicore.dev before the 60-day mark.
