MediCore General Hospital — Cardiology Department

Overview
The Cardiology Department at MediCore General Hospital provides comprehensive
care for patients with heart disease, vascular disorders, and related
conditions.  Our team of board-certified cardiologists and trained support
staff delivers evidence-based treatments in a compassionate environment.

Services Offered
- Electrocardiography (ECG/EKG)
- Echocardiography (transthoracic and transesophageal)
- Stress testing (exercise treadmill, nuclear, pharmacological)
- Holter and ambulatory blood-pressure monitoring
- Cardiac catheterisation and coronary angiography
- Percutaneous coronary intervention (PCI / stenting)
- Pacemaker implantation and remote monitoring
- Electrophysiology studies and ablation procedures
- Heart failure management clinic
- Lipid and preventive cardiology clinic

Location & Hours
The Cardiology outpatient clinic is located on the 3rd floor, East Wing
(Room 304–320).  Clinic hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
Emergency cardiology consultations are available 24 hours a day through
the main Emergency Department.

Referrals
All new outpatient appointments require a referral from a primary-care
physician or general practitioner.  Urgent referrals flagged by a GP are
typically seen within 48 hours.  Routine referrals are scheduled within
10 working days.
